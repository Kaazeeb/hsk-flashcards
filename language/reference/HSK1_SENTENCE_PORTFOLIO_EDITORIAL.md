# HSK 1 Sentence Portfolio Editorial Record

## Scope

This record documents the project-authored sentences added during the July 2026
HSK 1 sentence-card portfolio review. The product migration reduces the active
deck while preserving every published binding as immutable active or inactive
history.

The portfolio is optimized in this order:

1. natural HSK 1 Chinese and faithful English;
2. complete coverage of the 70 official HSK 1 grammar points;
3. at least two distinct exact contexts for every one of the 300 HSK 1
   vocabulary senses;
4. additional third contexts where they remain natural and useful;
5. card direction preference: Chinese to English, English to Chinese, then
   Chinese question and answer.

## Editorial Additions

Fourteen new sentences replace flawed, context-poor, or mechanically combined
legacy material. They focus on the vocabulary senses that could not retain two
quality contexts after the hard exclusions, and on natural replacements for
otherwise efficient but weak legacy cards.

The additions use only HSK 1 vocabulary. Each sentence has an approved English
translation, display pinyin, occurrence-ordered stable vocabulary relations, and
exact grammar relations where it deliberately demonstrates an official point.

## Review Basis

The additions were checked for idiomatic Chinese, translation fidelity,
lexical-sense resolution, HSK-level suitability, pinyin, and grammar-point fit.
Two independent agent passes reviewed the proposal set under the user's explicit
editorial exception. Mechanical catalog validation supplements those reviews but
does not replace them.

## Product Selection

The active deck combines:

- 226 stronger published HSK 1 sentence cards;
- 70 approved Grammar Study examples, one for every official HSK 1 grammar point;
- the 14 editorial sentences described above.

The resulting 310-card deck contains 225 Chinese-to-English cards, 80
English-to-Chinese cards, and 5 Chinese question-and-answer cards. Exact stable-ID
relations cover all 300 HSK 1 vocabulary senses at least twice, and 171 at least
three times.

The 64 rejected legacy cards remain registered as inactive historical bindings.
The two-context floor is established before third contexts are allocated, and a
numeric target never overrides sentence quality or lexical-sense accuracy.
