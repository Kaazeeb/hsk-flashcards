# HSK 3 Sentence Portfolio Editorial Record

## Scope

This record documents the project-authored sentences added during the July 2026
HSK 3 sentence-card portfolio review. The product migration reduces the active
deck while retaining every published card binding as an inactive historical row.

The portfolio is optimized in this order:

1. natural, level-appropriate Chinese and faithful English;
2. complete coverage of the 96 official HSK 3 grammar points;
3. broad coverage of the 500 HSK 3 vocabulary senses, with repeated contexts
   where they can be supplied without artificial sentence packing;
4. card direction preference: Chinese to English, English to Chinese, then
   Chinese question and answer.

## Editorial additions

Seventy-one new sentences replace weak or mechanically overloaded legacy
contexts. The first 35 address missing or unreliable senses; another 36 improve
pairing efficiency and establish a second natural context for scarce vocabulary.
They include the distinct syllabus senses of 得, 地, 过去, 还, 会, 站, 长, and
只. Each homograph occurrence is linked to its stable vocabulary ID.

The additions use only cumulative HSK 1-3 vocabulary. Each sentence has an
approved English translation, display pinyin, occurrence-ordered vocabulary
relations, and exact grammar relations where the sentence deliberately
demonstrates an official HSK 3 point.

## Review basis

The additions were checked for idiomatic Chinese, translation fidelity,
lexical-sense resolution, HSK level suitability, pinyin form, and grammar-point
fit. Approval was performed by the coding agent under the user's explicit
editorial exception. Mechanical catalog validation supplements this review but
does not substitute for it.

## Product selection

The active deck combines:

- 383 stronger published HSK 3 sentence cards;
- 96 approved Grammar Study examples, one for every official HSK 3 grammar point;
- the 71 editorial sentences described above.

The resulting 550-card deck contains 387 Chinese-to-English cards, 146
English-to-Chinese cards, and 17 Chinese question-and-answer cards. Exact
stable-ID relations cover all 500 HSK 3 vocabulary senses at least twice, and 257
at least three times.

Exact vocabulary occurrence links are used for coverage measurement. A numeric
coverage target never overrides sentence quality. The portfolio establishes the
two-context floor before distributing third contexts, so difficult items retain
room for natural examples instead of depending on artificial or redundant cards.
