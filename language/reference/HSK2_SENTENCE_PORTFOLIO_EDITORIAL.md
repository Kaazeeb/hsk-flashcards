# HSK 2 Sentence Portfolio Editorial Record

## Scope

This record documents the project-authored sentences added during the July 2026
HSK 2 sentence-card portfolio review. The product migration reduces the active
deck while preserving every published binding as immutable active or inactive
history.

The portfolio is optimized in this order:

1. natural cumulative HSK 1-2 Chinese and faithful English;
2. complete coverage of the 78 official HSK 2 grammar points;
3. at least two distinct exact contexts for every one of the 200 HSK 2
   vocabulary senses;
4. additional third contexts where they remain natural and useful;
5. card direction preference: Chinese to English, English to Chinese, then
   Chinese question and answer.

## Editorial Additions

Twenty-five new sentences replace flawed or mechanically packed legacy material
and resolve exact-sense gaps for homographs such as 点, 过, and 花. Compatible
targets are grouped only when they belong naturally to one scene, such as ordering
green tea and spending money or receiving flowers on a birthday.

The additions use only cumulative HSK 1-2 vocabulary. Each sentence has an
approved English translation, display pinyin, occurrence-ordered stable
vocabulary relations, and exact grammar relations where it deliberately
demonstrates an official point.

## Review Basis

The additions were checked for idiomatic Chinese, translation fidelity,
lexical-sense resolution, HSK-level suitability, pinyin, and grammar-point fit.
Two independent agent passes reviewed the proposal set under the user's explicit
editorial exception. Mechanical catalog validation supplements those reviews but
does not replace them.

## Product Selection

The active deck combines:

- 129 stronger published HSK 2 sentence cards;
- 78 approved Grammar Study examples, one for every official HSK 2 grammar point;
- the 25 editorial sentences described above.

The resulting 232-card deck contains 174 Chinese-to-English cards, 56
English-to-Chinese cards, and 2 Chinese question-and-answer cards. Exact stable-ID
relations cover all 200 HSK 2 vocabulary senses at least twice, and 58 at least
three times.

The selection excludes 49 hard rejects, 24 weak fallback cards, and every open
HSK 2 sentence issue. A 229-card alternative required seven weak fallbacks, so
the 232-card portfolio is retained as the smaller quality-feasible result.
